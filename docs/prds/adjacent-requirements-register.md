# Adjacent Requirements Register

This register protects website launch scope. First-class CRM, donor management, accounting, event-history, recurring-payment follow-up, supporter communications, and reporting requirements now live in [CRM, Donor Management, Accounting, and Supporter Communications](specs/007-crm-dms-ems.md).

Items here are not website launch requirements unless a PRD or later decision explicitly moves them into scope.

## Website Scope Guardrails

- A DFN-owned public donor self-service portal is out of website launch scope. Provider-hosted recurring payment management remains acceptable for launch.
- A QR scanning or live event check-in app is out of website launch scope. Operational check-in can use exports or a separate event tool.
- Peer-to-peer fundraiser self-registration, auto-generated fundraiser pages, approval workflow, and fundraiser publish notifications are out of website launch scope.
