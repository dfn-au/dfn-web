# DFN Website Rebuild

This glossary captures the domain language used by the DFN website rebuild.

## Language

**Country Site**:
A first-class public DFN website for one country. Each Country Site has its own visitor-facing domain, publishing scope, contact details, giving expectations, and jurisdiction-specific trust/receipt context.
_Avoid_: Country-aware content, regional variant

**Publishing Scope**:
The set of Country Sites where a piece of Authored Content is intended to appear. Some content is shared across Australia and New Zealand, while site-specific content appears on only one Country Site.
_Avoid_: Locale, market, audience segment

**Shared Content**:
Authored Content intended to appear on more than one Country Site as the same public item. A shared item can be published to all included Country Sites in one editorial action.
_Avoid_: Duplicate content, mirrored page

**Site-Specific Content**:
Authored Content intended to appear on exactly one Country Site. Site-Specific Content does not appear on another Country Site unless staff intentionally creates or shares a separate public item there.
_Avoid_: Regional override, localized field

**Country Site Attribution**:
The Country Site attached to supporter activity such as a donation, sponsorship, event registration, campaign gift, confirmation, notification, or reportable record. It reflects the public site where the visitor started the workflow.
_Avoid_: Country/currency choice, analytics region

**Country Payment Account**:
The payment account used by a Country Site for taking payments and reconciling funds. Australia and New Zealand use separate Country Payment Accounts.
_Avoid_: Shared payment account, payment metadata only

**Authored Content**:
Public-facing copy, media, labels, and approved configuration that DFN staff intentionally publish for visitors to read, browse, or choose from.
_Avoid_: Operational state, transaction data

**Operational State**:
Factual workflow status and records produced by supporter actions or staff operations, such as payment status, registrations, reservations, availability, and reporting records.
_Avoid_: Authored content, manually maintained progress
